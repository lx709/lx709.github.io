I am a Lecturer (Assistant Professor) in the School of Computer Science at the University of Bristol and a faculty member of the Machine Learning and Computer Vision (MaVi) group, working with Prof. [Dima Damen](https://www.bristol.ac.uk/people/person/Dima-Damen-d7924e62-0857-40fb-b9e4-9be99d801b7f/). Prior to joining Bristol, I held a Lecturer position at the University of Reading. Before that, I was a Postdoctoral Researcher at KAUST, working with Prof. [Mohamed Elhoseiny](https://scholar.google.com/citations?user=iRBUTOAAAAAJ&hl=en), and at NYU, working with Prof. [Yi Fang](https://scholar.google.com/citations?hl=en&user=j-cyhzwAAAAJ). My research focuses on multimodal large language models, computer vision, and remote sensing. I have published over 50 papers in top conferences and journals in computer vision and remote sensing, such as CVPR, ICCV, NeurIPS, TVCG, and TGRS, with <a href="https://scholar.google.com/citations?user=4Apl5FgAAAAJ&hl=en"><img src="https://img.shields.io/badge/Google%20Scholar-8000%20citations-9cf?logo=Google%20Scholar" /></a> citations on Google Scholar and an h-index of 28. I contributed to the well-known <a href="https://github.com/Vision-CAIR/MiniGPT-4">MiniGPT-4</a><img src="https://img.shields.io/github/stars/Vision-CAIR/MiniGPT-4?style=social" /> project, with over 3000 citations on Google Scholar. I served as a Guest Editor for several prestigious journals, including IEEE GRSM, IEEE JSTARS, and GSIS, and will serve as an Area Chair for ICLR 2026. I received the IEEE GRSS Early Career Award 2025 for high-impact research in computer vision methods in remote sensing applications.
